Our Story

TeachBot was born from a simple observation: teachers are overworked, under-supported, and expected to meet increasingly complex demands. We believe educators deserve smart tools that lighten the load and amplify their impact.
Founded by a passionate developer and education enthusiast, TeachBot is committed to solving real problems faced by teachers—starting with lesson planning and classroom support.

Our Mission

To empower educators everywhere with intelligent, accessible tools that enhance teaching and learning—one classroom at a time.

Our Vision

A future where every teacher, regardless of location or background, has access to AI-powered support that simplifies their work and helps them focus on what matters most: students.

Our Values

  • Quality Education for All: Inspired by UN SDG 4, we aim to make education smarter and more inclusive.
  • Teacher-Centered Innovation: We design with real classroom needs in mind.
  • Ethical AI: We believe in using technology responsibly, with transparency and fairness.

Meet The Founder

Wandile Ndlovu is a passionate software developer and certified TEFL teacher from Johannesburg, South Africa, driven by the belief that technology should empower—not replace educators. After spending time teaching English, Wandile experienced first-hand the challenges teachers face, especially those working full-time in physical classrooms. This sparked a deeper appreciation for how AI could meaningfully support lesson planning, engagement, and time management. With a background in web development and a growing passion for artificial intelligence, Wandile created TeachBot in response to the urgent need for smarter, simpler teaching tools that truly serve the educator.
